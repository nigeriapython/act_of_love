About Acts of Love International

Founded in 2005, Acts of Love International has been at the forefront of community development and poverty alleviation in Stockton-on-Tees and surrounding areas.

Our holistic approach addresses immediate needs while working on long-term solutions that empower individuals and strengthen communities.

Foodbank Impact

In 2022, our foodbank provided over 50,000 meals to families and individuals in need, a 35% increase from the previous year.

We've expanded our delivery services to reach isolated community members who cannot access our physical location.

Rhema House Renovation

Thanks to generous donations and volunteer efforts, we've completely renovated Rhema House, creating a safe and welcoming space.

The renovation included new facilities for counseling, community meetings, and educational workshops.
