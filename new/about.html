About Acts of Love International

Transforming communities through compassion, inclusion, and sustainable support for over 18 years

Our Story

Founded in 2006 and registered in 2007, Acts of Love International is based at Rhema House in Stockton-on-Tees. Our community has faced some of the toughest social challenges in the UK - poverty, unemployment, crime and poor health. Yet it is also a place of strength, resilience, and rich cultural diversity.

For over 18 years, we've been working to transform these challenges into opportunities for growth and connection. Our neighbourhood is filled with incredible people who inspire us with their strength and resilience every day.
