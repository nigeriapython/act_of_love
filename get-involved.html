Volunteer Opportunities

Our volunteers are the heart of our organisation. Whether you can give a few hours a week or make a regular commitment, your time and skills make a real difference in our community.

Foodbank Volunteer

Help sort, pack, and distribute food parcels to families in need. Support our Wednesday and Friday foodbank sessions.

Time Commitment: 2-4 hours weekly

Skills Needed: Organisation, empathy, teamwork

Sports & Activities Volunteer

Assist with our Friday and Saturday sports sessions. Help set up activities and encourage participation.

Time Commitment: 3-4 hours weekly

Skills Needed: Enthusiasm, basic sports knowledge

General Support Volunteer

Help with various tasks around Rhema House including administration, cleaning, and community outreach.

Time Commitment: Flexible

Skills Needed: Various - we'll match to your strengths
