Rhema House: Our Community Hub

The Heart of Our Operations

Rhema House is the heart of our operations—a place of safety, learning, and opportunity for the entire community.

This vibrant community hub serves as the central location for all our programs and initiatives, bringing people together from all backgrounds and walks of life.

Our Facilities

Rhema House offers diverse spaces to serve our community's needs

Upstairs Facilities

Our upstairs area houses multiple spaces designed for learning, gathering, and support services.

Meeting Rooms

Spaces for community groups, counseling sessions, and small gatherings

Classrooms

Dedicated spaces for ESOL classes, training workshops, and educational programs

Foodbank

Distribution center providing essential nutrition to families in need

Clothing Store

Quality clothing available for those who need it most

Our Future Vision

Rhema House is poised to become the first dedicated, purpose-built space for the BAME and wider community in Teesside, with high-quality facilities and cultural understanding at its core.

Transformative Impact

Our vision extends beyond physical spaces to creating genuine opportunities for connection, growth, and empowerment across all communities.
