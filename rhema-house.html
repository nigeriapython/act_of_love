Rhema House: Our Community Hub

The heart of our operations, a place of safety, learning, and opportunity for the entire community

Welcome to Rhema House

Rhema House is the vibrant community hub that serves as the central location for all our programs and initiatives, bringing people together from all backgrounds and walks of life.

Located at the heart of Stockton-on-Tees, Rhema House provides a safe, welcoming space where community members can access essential services, participate in activities, and build meaningful connections.
