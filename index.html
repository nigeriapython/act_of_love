About Acts of Love International

Founded in 2006, Acts of Love International has been at the forefront of community development and poverty alleviation in Stockton-on-Tees and surrounding areas.

We are a registered charity (Charity No. 1119687) set up to support people from ethnic minority backgrounds, living in deprived wards to get access to sport and activities which increase health well-being, mental health, confidence and knowledge and to decrease isolation.

Our Charitable Objectives

  1. To relieve sickness and hardship and to promote and preserve good health by the provision of goods or services of any kind, including through the provision of counselling and sporting activities.
  2. To advance education in such ways and in such parts of the United Kingdom.
