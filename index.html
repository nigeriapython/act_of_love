About Acts of Love International

Founded in 2005, Acts of Love International has been at the forefront of community development and poverty alleviation in Stockton-on-Tees and surrounding areas.

Our holistic approach addresses immediate needs while working on long-term solutions that empower individuals and strengthen communities.
